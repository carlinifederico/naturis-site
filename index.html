"Technology is nature too..."

A live immersive experience where code and ecosystem merge into a single pulse. NATURIS invites us to reconnect the human, the digital, and the organic in one continuous flow of balance and evolution.

by Jorge Tereso, Federico Carlini and Fernando Maldonado

NATURIS imagines a world where technology is not outside of nature, but another way it grows. Like roots, rivers, or lungs, digital systems expand and branch, forming patterns of life.

For centuries they were used to extract and destroy, but as they become more intelligent, they also become more careful, sustainable, and organic.

This project stands beyond good or bad: it does not resist or celebrate, but invites us to see the union. What begins as turbulence becomes rhythm and balance, until it is clear there are not two worlds, but one continuous flow.

NATURIS is an invitation to reconnect, to evolve technology so it can live as part of nature.

"Technology is nature too, growing with the same pulse as roots, rivers, and lungs."

"At first, the encounter is turbulent, overflowing with raw, unstable energy."

"Code is not separate from nature, it is another way life organizes itself."

"Out of this storm comes rhythm, balance, and a strange sense of harmony."

Room 1

"Let go and fall"

Immersive Experience
  • Gravity is reversed: the human world collapses while nature holds its ground.
  • Falling objects reveal a fragile dependence on space and balance.
  • Two realities mirror each other, exposing how existence relies on context.
Room 2

"All is connected"

Immersive Experience
  • Bodies react subtly to the presence of others.
  • Invisible networks expand through proximity and contact.
  • A living system emerges, where each movement affects the whole.
Room 3

"Blind shapes"

Immersive Experience
  • Different speeds coexist within a shared temporal field.
  • Movements align without awareness, guided by a common rhythm.
  • Time stretches and compresses, revealing an underlying synchronization.
Room 4

"Traces of the footprints"

Immersive Experience
  • Human paths echo the logic of natural growth.
  • Movements across land resemble data flowing through networks.
  • Territory becomes a record of shared behaviors between life and systems.
Room 5

"The shape of music"

Immersive Experience
  • Sound transforms into evolving visual structures.
  • Birdsong becomes data that shapes space and color.
  • Music is revealed as architecture in motion.
Room 6

"Memories of water"

Immersive Experience
  • Water flows as a carrier of connections across ecosystems.
  • Streams and currents trace invisible relationships between living systems.
  • Movement of water becomes a memory that links all forms of life.

Closing Statement

Our role is not to choose sides, but to recognize ourselves as part of this hybrid ecosystem, to learn to listen to it, and to move in harmony with its rhythm.
