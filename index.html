"Technology is nature too..."

A live immersive experience where code and ecosystem merge into a single pulse. NATURIS invites us to reconnect the human, the digital, and the organic in one continuous flow of balance and evolution.

by Jorge Tereso, Federico Carlini and Fernando Maldonado

NATURIS imagines a world where technology is not outside of nature, but another way it grows. Like roots, rivers, or lungs, digital systems expand and branch, forming patterns of life.

For centuries they were used to extract and destroy, but as they become more intelligent, they also become more careful, sustainable, and organic.

This project stands beyond good or bad: it does not resist or celebrate, but invites us to see the union. What begins as turbulence becomes rhythm and balance, until it is clear there are not two worlds, but one continuous flow.

NATURIS is an invitation to reconnect, to evolve technology so it can live as part of nature.

"Technology is nature too, growing with the same pulse as roots, rivers, and lungs."

"At first, the encounter is turbulent, overflowing with raw, unstable energy."

"Code is not separate from nature, it is another way life organizes itself."

"Out of this storm comes rhythm, balance, and a strange sense of harmony."
